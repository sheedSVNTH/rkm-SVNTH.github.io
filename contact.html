Frequently Asked Questions

See below common questions and information regarding our Policy(s).

SIZING

1. WHAT SIZE SHOULD I ORDER?

Garments are designed for a slim-athletic, form fitting wear. If you prefer a "looser fit," order (1) size above your "true size."

2. DO SHIRTS/TANKS FIT WOMEN?

While most designs are Uni-sex and can be worn by Women, the sizing designs are based on US Conventional Men's Sizing. For Women's sizing, we suggest ordering (1) size below your "true size" for our shirts/tanks.

We are in the process of developing women's products for future release.

SHIPPING

1. HOW LONG WILL MY ORDER TAKE TO RECEIVE?

All U.S. Domestic orders will be shipped via FedEx, UPS, or USPS depending on order size and shipping destination. We will work to process your order within 2-3 days of receiving your order. Once product ships, shipping transit duration is at the discretion of the shipping vendor (FedEx, UPS, or USPS).
