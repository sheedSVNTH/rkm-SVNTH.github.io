About Us

Seventh Level Apparel was founded in 2015. The philosophy of achieving the “Seventh Level” came from the idea of the place you must go mentally when training to push yourself to continue and endure the pain to grow. An extreme focus that removes you from the current world into another state. In this place, no other matter exists, its simply your energy, rage, raw emotion and power that you harness to push to another level.


After further brainstorming, we realized, this idea was too narrow and that taking a broader approach would be more meaningful. That’s when we developed the “7 Levels.”


Seventh Level is about the journey of self-development with the ultimate goal of finding fulfillment in life.
